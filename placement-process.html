Placement Process

  1. Home
  2. Placement Process

Given below is the procedure for placements:

1.The placement ofﬁce shall communicate, network and build relations with potential recruiters. Students seeking placement shall register for the campus placements.

2. The placement ofﬁce conﬁrms his/her registration, participates in pre-placement visits to the companies and conducts grooming and training of the students for better placements.

3. Send invitation to potential recruiters to visit the Institute and procure relevant details regarding the ﬁnal placements. Respond to recruiters’ intention to participate.

4. The placement team communicates the dates for campus visit for each recruiter by considering factors like student preference, job proﬁle, etc.

5. Companies are encouraged to give pre-placement talks before the commencement of recruitment process.

6. The companies conduct different rounds of selection process (aptitude test, group discussion, personal interview, HR and others) and inform the names of selected students to the placement department, which in turn shall inform the concerned students of the same.

7. The placement ofﬁce conducts general follow-up, joining formalities and other administrative activities.
